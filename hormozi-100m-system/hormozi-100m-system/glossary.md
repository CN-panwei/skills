# Glossary — $100M Business System Key Terms

## Offer Architecture
- **Money Model**: A sequence of offers that turns strangers into customers, maximizes their spend, catches those who say no, and keeps them buying. Combines four offer types in order.
- **Grand Slam Offer**: An offer so good people feel stupid saying no. Achieved when all four components of the Value Equation are optimized.
- **Core Offer**: What you sell in exchange for money. Your primary product or service.
- **Lead Magnet**: A complete solution to a narrow problem, typically free or low-cost, that reveals another problem solved by your Core Offer.
- **Value Equation**: (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood of Achievement) / (Perceived Time Delay × Perceived Effort & Sacrifice). The formula for offer irresistibility.
- **Value Stack**: The practice of listing every deliverable, bonus, and guarantee to make total value far exceed the asking price.

## Offer Types (Money Models)
- **Attraction Offer**: Front-end offer (free, $1, or discount) that turns strangers into customers and generates cash to cover acquisition costs.
- **Upsell Offer**: Offer made after a purchase to solve the next revealed problem. The primary profit driver.
- **Downsell Offer**: Offer made after a rejection — changes WHAT they get or HOW they pay, never just discounting.
- **Continuity Offer**: Recurring-revenue offer (subscription, membership, consumable reorder, retainer) that keeps customers paying month after month.

## Attraction Offer Subtypes
- **Win Your Money Back**: Pay now, earn refund through results/actions. ~10% ask for refunds; extra customers more than offset it.
- **Giveaway**: Grand prize attracts leads; non-winners get promotional discount. "Many will enter, many will win."
- **Decoy Offer**: Free/discounted decoy alongside premium offer. Contrast makes premium irresistible.
- **Buy X Get Y Free**: Reframe pricing so free items outnumber paid items. "Buy 1, get 2 free."
- **Pay Less Now or Pay More Later**: Discount for immediate payment vs. full price later with guarantee.

## Upsell Offer Subtypes
- **Classic Upsell**: Solve the problem your previous offer just revealed. "You can't have X without Y."
- **Menu Upsell**: Unselling + Prescription + A/B choice + Card On File. "Chocolate or vanilla?"
- **Anchor Upsell**: Present 5-10x premium first → get "The Gasp" → present target offer at 1/5 the price.
- **Rollover Upsell**: Credit previous purchase toward a more expensive next offer.

## Downsell Offer Subtypes
- **Payment Plan Downsell**: Break lump sum into installments. Charge more overall.
- **Trial With Penalty**: Full access for trial period; auto-charge if not cancelled.
- **Feature Downsell**: Remove non-core features and reduce price accordingly.

## Leads & Advertising
- **Lead**: A person you can contact.
- **Engaged Lead**: A person who shows interest in what you sell (gives contact info, follows you, replies to campaigns). The true output of advertising.
- **Warm Audience**: People who have given you permission to contact them (followers, customers, contacts).
- **Cold Audience**: Strangers who have NOT given you permission to contact them.
- **Core Four**: The four ways to get leads: Warm Outreach, Cold Outreach, Content (Create), Paid Ads.
- **Lead Getter**: A person who generates leads on your behalf — customer, employee, agency, or affiliate.
- **Rule of 100**: Send 100 personalized cold messages per day as minimum viable cold outreach effort.
- **BAMFAM**: Book A Meeting From A Meeting. End every appointment by scheduling the next one.
- **CTA (Call To Action)**: Tells the audience what to do next + reasons to do it RIGHT NOW (scarcity, urgency, any reason).

## Value Equation Components
- **Dream Outcome**: The ultimate result the customer desires. Score 1-100, maximize.
- **Perceived Likelihood of Achievement**: How likely they believe success is. Score 1-100, maximize.
- **Perceived Time Delay**: How long until they experience results. Score 0-1, minimize.
- **Perceived Effort & Sacrifice**: How much work they believe is required. Score 0-1, minimize.
- **Offer Score**: (Dream × Success) / (Time × Effort). Target 1,000,000+ for Grand Slam.

## AI-Powered Creation
- **Hormozi-fier Prompt**: ChatGPT prompt that scores your offer on the Value Equation and provides optimization advice.
- **USP (Unique Selling Point)**: Distinct advantages or features that differentiate your offer from competitors.
- **UVP (Unique Value Proposition)**: Communicates the specific benefits your offer provides to your target audience.
- **A/B Testing**: Comparing two versions of an offer element (headline, image, CTA) to determine which performs better.
