# Chapter 7: The Core Four — Four Ways to Get Leads

## Core Idea
There are only **four ways** one person can let other people know about something. Combine two audience types (Warm/Cold) with two communication modes (1-to-1/1-to-many) and you get the Core Four: Warm Outreach, Cold Outreach, Content (Create), Paid Ads.

## Frameworks Introduced

### The 2×2 Matrix
| | 1-to-1 (Private) | 1-to-Many (Public) |
|---|---|---|
| **Warm** (know you) | Warm Outreach | Content / Posting |
| **Cold** (don't know you) | Cold Outreach | Paid Ads |

### Method 1: Warm Outreach
- **What**: Contact people who already know you — friends, family, followers, current/past customers, contacts
- **How**: Phone call, text, DM, email, in-person
- **Key advantage**: Trust already exists
- **Best practice**: Ask warm contacts to share/forward — "If this isn't for you, do you know someone it IS for?"

### Method 2: Cold Outreach
- **What**: Contact strangers who haven't given you permission — bought lists, scraped contacts, social media DMs
- **Key difference from warm**: No trust. Must build it instantly.
- **How**: Personalize every message. Reference something specific about them. Lead with value, not your pitch.
- **The Math**: Cold outreach is a numbers game. Figure out your conversion rate → scale the volume.
- **Rule of 100**: Send 100 personalized cold messages per day. If you can't convert after 100/day for 30 days, fix your offer or targeting.

### Method 3: Content (Create)
- **What**: Post publicly so many people see it at once — social media, podcasts, YouTube, blogs, newsletters
- **Key advantage**: Build trust at scale. Every piece of content is a salesperson working 24/7.
- **The Compound Effect**: Old content continues attracting leads forever. Audience compounds over time.
- **Content as Lead Magnet**: Each piece should solve a micro-problem and point to your lead magnet or core offer.
- **Content Creates Tools for Salespeople**: When prospects see your content, they're pre-sold before the sales conversation begins.

### Method 4: Paid Ads
- **What**: Pay platforms to put your offer in front of cold audiences at scale
- **Key advantage**: Fastest way to scale lead volume — dial up spend, get more leads
- **Four Problems Paid Ads Must Solve**:
  1. **Who to show** (Targeting): Demographics, interests, behaviors, lookalikes
  2. **What to show** (Creative): Images, videos, copy that stops the scroll
  3. **Where to send** (Landing Page): Where clicks convert to engaged leads
  4. **How much to pay** (Bidding): Cost per click, cost per lead, ROAS targets
- **The Efficiency Loop**: Better targeting → better creative → better landing page → lower cost per lead → more leads per dollar → scale spend profitably

## Key Concepts
- **Warm audiences** = people who gave you permission to contact them
- **Cold audiences** = strangers who have NOT given you permission
- **1-to-1 communication** = private (phone, email, DM)
- **1-to-Many communication** = public (social post, podcast, ad)
- **Automation ≠ new channel**: Emailing 10,000 people at once is still 1-to-1, just faster
- **Paid ads are the ultimate leverage**: Spend $1 → get $36 back (36:1 ROI) when dialed in

## Mental Models
- Start with **Warm Outreach** (easiest, trust exists) → add **Content** (builds trust at scale) → layer **Cold Outreach** (fill gaps) → scale with **Paid Ads** (fastest growth)
- Think of each method as a **funnel opening**: wider at the top (paid ads, content) → narrower (warm/cold outreach) → core offer conversion
- **Content is the bridge** between cold and warm — strangers consume your content and become warm leads

## Anti-patterns
- **Only doing paid ads**: If your offer and messaging don't convert through warm outreach, paid ads will just lose money faster
- **Cold outreach without personalization**: "Spray and pray" destroys your brand and reply rates
- **Creating content without a CTA**: Content that doesn't point to your lead magnet or offer is entertainment, not marketing
- **Not tracking cost per lead**: If you don't know what a lead costs, you can't improve it

## Key Takeaways
1. There are only four ways to get leads — master all four, and you'll never have a lead problem again.
2. Warm Outreach is the easiest starting point — you already have trust, use it.
3. Cold Outreach: Personalize, lead with value, scale volume. 100 messages/day is the minimum viable effort.
4. Content: Every post is a salesperson. Old content works forever. Compound interest of attention.
5. Paid Ads: Fastest way to scale, but only after your offer converts through the other three methods first.
6. The sequence matters: Prove your offer works with warm leads → prove it works with cold leads → prove it works at scale with paid ads.
7. Combine methods: Content warms up cold audiences → warm outreach follows up → paid ads accelerate what's already working.
